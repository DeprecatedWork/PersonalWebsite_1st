Hack24

Hack24 Website

Hack24 is a 24 Hour coding competition organised by Tech Nottingham. I arrived and joined two strangers to form the team Jodi and The Jess'.

We entered the MidlandHR Challenge to create a website where employees could rate their managers and users could retrieve information about company fit, gender ratio etc.

You can read MidlandHR's report on the event here.

BrumHack 2.0

BrumHack Website

BrumHack 2.0 was a student hackathon based at The University of Birmingham.

For this hack, our two man team retained data from the .gov websites on salaries of different professions across the UK for men and women. We then attempted to display this data on Google Maps to show the difference in pay between men and women.
